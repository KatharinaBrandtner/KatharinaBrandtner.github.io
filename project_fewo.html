accomodation website

The website for Ferienwohnungen Brandtner was outdated and no longer met modern user expectations. The goal was to create a fresh, user-friendly design that makes booking easier while digitally reflecting the charm of the vacation apartments. By integrating ”Easy Booking”, the reservation process was optimized, allowing guests to book quickly and hassle-free.

services

new website visual identity logo & branding mobile first redesign UI design web development

challange

The old website was visually and technically outdated, which negatively impacted the user experience and booking rates. It lacked an intuitive booking system, and the design did not reflect the quality and coziness of the apartments. The goal was to modernize the website, improve usability, and streamline the booking process.

my role & impact

design

I developed a modern and clean layout, focusing on high-quality visuals and intuitive navigation. The color scheme and typography were carefully chosen to match the welcoming atmosphere of the apartments.

Development

The website was optimized for performance and responsiveness, ensuring smooth functionality across all devices.

Booking System

By integrating Easy Booking, the reservation process became seamless, allowing direct online bookings without unnecessary steps.

design system

Logo & Brand Identity

the geometric shape represents a mountain, symbolizing the Alpine region. When rotated 90 degrees, it forms an "F"- standing for the words “Ferienwohnungen” and “franz”.

Color Palette & typography

The colors enhances the connection to nature while maintaining a modern and inviting appearance. The typography choice balances modern usability with a touch of tradition.

Dynamics and variety

To create a cohesive digital experience, the components follow a harmonized style inspired by nature. the imagery mood is Warm with soft tones, a mix of interior and outdoor shots.

key learnings

1. Simplicity increases conversions
A streamlined booking process and intuitive navigation significantly increase the likelihood of guests completing their reservations.

2. Visual quality matters
A modern design with high-quality images and a structured layout enhances the brand image and makes the website more inviting.

3. The right platform makes all the difference
Integrating Easy Booking automated many tasks, reducing administrative workload and improving the user experience.
